Buying Guide for Real Estate Agents

Must-Have Features:

  • 8+ hour battery - You're away from outlets all day between showings and client meetings.
  • Lightweight under 3.5 lbs - You're carrying this between showings, open houses, and the office.
  • Good webcam - Virtual showings are standard now. 1080p makes a visible difference.
  • Reliable Wi-Fi 6 - Connecting to coffee shop networks, hotspots, and office Wi-Fi all day.
  • SSD for fast boot - No waiting around when you open your laptop at a listing.

Nice to Have:

  • LTE or 5G option - Never depend on finding Wi-Fi. Always connected for MLS and CRM access.
  • Touchscreen - Digital signatures and showing listings to clients directly on screen.
  • HDMI port - Connecting to the office TV for team presentations and market updates.
