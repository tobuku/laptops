What to Look for in a Video Editing Laptop

  • RAM: 16GB minimum. 32GB if you edit 4K with heavy color grading and effects.
  • Display: OLED or 100% DCI-P3 IPS for accurate color. You can't trust your grade on a cheap panel.
  • Storage: 1TB minimum. 4K footage averages 25–80GB per hour depending on codec.
  • GPU: Dedicated GPU (RTX 3050+) for GPU-accelerated effects in Premiere and DaVinci. Integrated is okay for Final Cut Pro on Mac.
  • CPU: AMD Ryzen 7/9 or Intel Core i7/i9 — both excellent. Apple M3/M3 Pro for macOS workflows.
